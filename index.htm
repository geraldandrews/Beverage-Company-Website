About BBC

Bluefield Beverage was a Kroger owned Fortune 500 beverage manufacturing and distribution bottling company located in Bluefield, Va. The team and staff at Bluefield Beverage were committed to producing and distributing the highest quality beverage products locally and nationally for their clients, retailers, and grocery chains from the time they opened in 1983 until they closed in 2012. Bluefield Beverage staffed over 180 associates and produced and distributed beverage products for long time satisfied clients and grocery chains such as Kroger, Piggly Wiggly, Food City, Dollar General, Rocky Top and various other small chain grocery stores.
