About Us

Bluefield Beverage is a Kroger owned Fortune 500 beverage manufacturing and distribution bottling company located in Bluefield, Va. The team and staff at Bluefield Beverage have been committed to producing and distributing the highest quality beverage products locally and nationally for our clients, retailers, Kroger stores, and other grocery chains since 1983. We currently staff over 180 associates and produce beverage products for our long time satisfied clients and grocery chains such as Kroger, Piggly Wiggly, Food City, Dollar General, Rocky Top and looking to expand. Contact us for your beverage manufacturing and distribution needs.
